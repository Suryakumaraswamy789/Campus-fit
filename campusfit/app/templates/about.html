About CampusFit

Our Mission: To create a safer, more effective, and more connected fitness environment for all college students. We believe that fitness should be accessible, and knowledge should be shared.

CampusFit was born during a hackathon with a simple idea: many junior students are intimidated by the gym and don't know where to start, while many senior students have valuable knowledge to share. This platform bridges that gap.

By connecting learners with experienced mentors on campus, we foster a community of peer-to-peer learning. This not only helps prevent injuries caused by improper form but also builds confidence and encourages a lifelong habit of health and wellness.

Thank you for being a part of our community!
